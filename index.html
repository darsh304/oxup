About Token

The key highlight of OXUP is its token reflection mechanism. This mechanism is designed to reward OXUP holders simply for owning and holding the cryptocurrency. With each transaction conducted on the OXUP network, a percentage of the transaction fee is automatically distributed back to the existing token holders. This distribution, known as token reflections, occurs in real-time and is proportional to the number of OXUP tokens held by each individual.

By implementing this unique approach, OXUP incentivizes its community to retain their holdings, fostering a strong and loyal investor base. Token reflections offer a passive income stream for OXUP holders, as they continue to earn additional tokens with every transaction on the network. This mechanism not only rewards long-term investors but also encourages active participation and engagement within the OXUP ecosystem.
